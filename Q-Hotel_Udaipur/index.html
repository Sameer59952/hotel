      A Royal Retreat - One of The Best Resorts in Udaipur

      Historically referred to as the residence of the Maharaja of Pilibhit, The Naini Retreat Hotel in Udaipur is among the best resorts in Udaipur near lake. This 5-star hotel in Udaipur hotel is situated on the picturesque Ayarpatta Slopes, providing a breathtaking view of the Naini Lake at an altitude of 1,995 meters. This heritage hotel in Udaipur has a strikingly beautiful palatial building, that embodies an old-world charm along with visible impressions of contemporary luxury. If you are wondering what awaits you here, then you will be glad to know that we offer royally furnished bedrooms, the best luxury rooms and suites in Udaipur, that exude new age grandeur and hospitality. Our friendly staff are ever ready to assist you throughout your stay.

      For an intimate and exceptional dining experience, we serve a variety of Indian, Mughlai, Chinese and Continental cuisines in our dining rooms that overlook lush green landscapes and winding valleys. We take pride in offering the best Kumaon food for our guests. Not to disappoint our business and corporate guests, luxury resort in Udaipur provides exclusive services to host business conferences and meetings in our spacious conference halls, the best conference halls in Udaipur.

      The Himalayan hotel re-awakens the pleasures of living - you are welcome to take a trek into the mountains, go on a golfing date at the regal hundred-year-old Raj Bhawan Golf Course, enjoy a special cultural evening featuring performances by traditional Kumaoni artists or take a yacht ride at the highest sailing club in the world.
